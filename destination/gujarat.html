Discover Gujarat

Land of legends, lions, and timeless traditions

About Gujarat

Gujarat is a state along the western coast of India known for its vibrant culture, rich heritage, and economic vitality. From Gir National Park to the Rann of Kutch, Gujarat offers experiences that are both wild and spiritual.

Key Highlights

  • Statue of Unity – World's tallest statue
  • Home to Asiatic Lions (Gir)
  • Colorful Navratri festivals
  • White Desert Festival
  • Renowned for vegetarian cuisine
  • Famous for bandhani and handicrafts

Must-Visit Places

Statue of Unity

Dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, it's the world's tallest statue at 182 meters.

View on Map

Jamnagar Palace

The Jamnagar Palace, located in the heart of Jamnagar city, is a royal residence that showcases the grandeur of Gujarat's royal past, with beautiful architecture and historic significance.

View on Map

Rann of Kutch

Famous for the Rann Utsav, the Rann of Kutch is a vast white salt desert offering mesmerizing landscapes, cultural festivals, and the chance to witness the unique beauty of Gujarat.
