Queen of the Hills — where colonial charm meets Himalayan bliss

About Shimla

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is a scenic hill station famous for its colonial architecture, snow-capped mountains, and pine-covered hills. Once the summer capital of British India, it's a favorite getaway for nature lovers and honeymooners.

Key Highlights

  • Himalayan views and snowfall
  • UNESCO Kalka-Shimla toy train
  • British colonial architecture
  • Dense cedar and pine forests
  • Popular winter destination
  • Trekking and adventure sports

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

March to June for pleasant weather, December to February for snow.

Average Temperature

Daytime Range: 12°C to 22°C
Nighttime Range: 0°C to 10°C

Pricing

Expect higher prices during peak tourist seasons. Bargain where possible.
