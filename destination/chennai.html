Discover Chennai

A coastal blend of tradition, culture, and technology

About Chennai

Chennai, the capital of Tamil Nadu, is a vibrant city on the Bay of Bengal. Known for its deep-rooted traditions, colonial history, and booming IT sector, Chennai is a unique mix of heritage and modern life.

Key Highlights

  • Marina Beach – India's longest urban beach
  • Colonial architecture & churches
  • Birthplace of Carnatic music & Bharatanatyam
  • Hub of medical tourism
  • Famous for filter coffee and South Indian cuisine
  • Major IT and automobile industries

Must-Visit Places

Marina Beach

A scenic stretch of coastline ideal for morning walks, sunsets, and street snacks.

View on Map

St. Mary's Church

St. Mary's Church, an architectural gem within Fort St. George complex, will transport you back in time. Established in 1679 AD, this magnificent Anglican English church holds incredible stories and remarkable features that set it apart from others.

View on Map

Mettur Dam

Positioned on the gigantic Kaveri River, the Mettur Dam in Tamil Nadu is one of the most iconic symbols of human ingenuity and a source of life for this area.
