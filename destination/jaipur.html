About Jaipur

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is the capital of Rajasthan. Known for its rich history, stunning forts, palaces, and vibrant culture, Jaipur offers a royal experience with its beautiful architecture and heritage.

Key Highlights

  • Amber Fort – Majestic hilltop fort
  • City Palace – Royal residence
  • Hawa Mahal – The Palace of Winds
  • Jantar Mantar – Ancient observatory
  • Colorful bazaars and handicrafts
  • Delicious Rajasthani cuisine

Must-Visit Places

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

October to March – pleasant weather for sightseeing and festivals.

Average Temperature

Daytime Range: 20°C to 30°C
Nighttime Range: 10°C to 20°C

Pricing

Be mindful of higher prices for tourists—bargain politely at markets.
